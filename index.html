The New Financing Architecture

The era of single-streamer full commissions is ending. To fund a premium non-fiction series in 2026, we must utilize a **"Hybrid Consortium"** model. This approach reduces risk for distributors and unlocks capital from non-media sectors.

Current Commissioning Landscape (2025-26)

Direct commissions are shrinking. Co-productions are rising.

1. The "Artemis Grant" Route

Leveraging 2026 NSF/NASA STEM engagement funds specifically earmarked for "Future Workforce Development." This covers 30-40% of the budget as "soft money."

2. Private Sector Brand Equity

Partnerships with "New Space" companies (Blue Origin, Axiom Space) or Tech Toys (LEGO Technic). They don't want ads; they want *integration* (e.g., using their real designs).

3. International Pre-Sales

Selling terrestrial rights to public broadcasters (BBC, NHK, CBC) who mandate E/I content, before approaching US Streamers for the "Premium Window."
