Project: Horizon 2026

Strategic Market Analysis for Next-Gen Non-Fiction Space Programming

Target: Gen Alpha Focus: Survival STEM

Executive Summary

The 2026 media landscape shows a critical saturation point in fantasy-based sci-fi, creating a significant void in high-stakes, reality-based STEM content.

The 10-13 "Pre-Teen" demographic demands content that respects their intelligence—moving away from "whimsical exploration" toward "survival science" and "engineering reality."
